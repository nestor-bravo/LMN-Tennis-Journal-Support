Privacy

Tennis Journal stores all of your data locally on your device. The app does not collect, transmit, or share any personal information.

There are no accounts, no sign-ups, no analytics, and no third-party tracking. Any backups you export are files that you control, and they are never sent anywhere. If you delete the app, your data is removed with it.
